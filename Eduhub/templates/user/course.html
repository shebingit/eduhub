On The Job Training

Welcome to Altos Eduhub - the premier software training company in Kerala, and a subsidiary of Altos Technologies - one of the leading software development companies in Kochi. We offer a range of on-the-job training programs in Python, React JS, Front End Development, REST API, and Digital Marketing. TRAINING PROGRAMS

Our training programs are six months in duration, with the first three months focused on fundamental training and the remaining three months dedicated to practical work on live projects. Upon successful completion of the training, you will receive an experience certificate. For Python training, we also offer guaranteed placement at Altos Technologies, while for other programs, we provide placement assistance.

To ensure the best possible training experience for our candidates, we have implemented several measures. As a part of our training program, we provide candidates with access to Altos core software, where they can update their tasks and track their work status efficiently. Additionally, we conduct weekly meetings for each of the candidates to evaluate their work and performance. We believe in providing continuous support to our candidates and offer a doubt clearing section that is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
