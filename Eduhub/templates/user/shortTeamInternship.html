Short Term
Internship

Altos Technologies offers a short-term internship program for studying graduates to gain real-world experience and academic credit. Our program provides flexibility with options ranging from 2 days to 1 month. Interns will have the opportunity to work on real-world projects, collaborate with experienced professionals, and receive feedback from mentors. The program also provides the chance to build valuable connections and gain a competitive edge in the job market. Upon completion, interns will receive a valuable certificate highlighting their skills and experience. Our program is designed to enhance both academic and professional careers.
